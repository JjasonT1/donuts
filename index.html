About

Imperial Donuts is an independently owned and family run business that specializes in the creation of artisan donuts. Established in early 2013, we have been blown away by the unexpected success and popularity of our carefully crafted donut. Our donuts are freshly prepared each day prior to sun-rise and amongst our most popular donuts include the honey-glazed donut, the jam-filled donut and our ice-glazed donut.

Currently we make 117 different types of donuts on a daily-basis and have two stores in the Toronto area, one store in Montreal and one store in Chicago. Definitely pop by one of our locations to try at least one of our donuts but if you can't make it, don't worry! We've recently introduced both a catering service for those special occassions such as weddings & birthday's and have also recently introduced a delivery service for our 'Suprise Donut Collection'box which contained 6 different donut flavours.
